Colors

After trying many different combinations of colors, I decided to choose different shadows of dark navy blue and sand color. Blue is considered very professional and using light beige instead of white makes this mix a bit unusual. I've checked the contrast between those colors using Adobe's contrast analyzer and got contrast ratio equal almost to 13:1 (minimum color contrast ratio for legible text is 3:1).

I wanted dark theme to be the default one, but a user can use a toggle button located in the navigation to toggle between dark and light modes (see mockups above).
